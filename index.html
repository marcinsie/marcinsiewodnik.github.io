To do so I studied also Finance and Accounting, ACCA specialization at Poznan University of Economics. During this time I gathered unique knowledge which is very helpfung in today's changing environment. But the most important thing is putting these things into practise. But at some point I decided that I want to focus on programming. So I started learing intensively java and javascript. Very helpful and developing were udemy courses.
